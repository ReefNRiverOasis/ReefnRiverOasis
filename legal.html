Reef & River Oasis Policies

Customer Service Policy

Quality Assurance: At Reef & River Oasis, we are committed to providing the highest quality products and exceptional customer service. Our staff is knowledgeable and ready to assist you with any queries or issues you may have.

Communication: Customers can contact us via phone, email, or in-store for any support or inquiries. We aim to respond to all queries within 24 hours. For immediate assistance, please visit our store during business hours.

Returns and Refunds Policy

Returns: We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange, provided the item is in its original condition with the original packaging. Live fish and other perishable items are excluded from this policy due to their sensitive nature.

Refunds: Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days upon receipt of the returned item. Proof of purchase, such as a receipt or invoice, is required for all refunds and exchanges. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Exceptions: If a fish or aquatic life becomes ill within 7 days of purchase, we will offer a replacement or store credit. This health guarantee does not cover neglect, improper care, or unsuitable environmental conditions.

Animal Welfare Policy

Care Standards: We ensure all our fish and aquatic life are sourced from reputable suppliers and are kept in optimal conditions in our store. We adhere to strict care guidelines to ensure the health and well-being of all our aquatic life.

Health Guarantee: We provide a health guarantee for all our fish. If a fish becomes ill within 7 days of purchase, we will offer a replacement or store credit. Please provide a water sample and details of your tank setup for a thorough assessment.
